Sensors

Despite having no brain, jellyfish can detect light and navigate their environment using a simple nerve net.

Biotechnology

The jelly-like substance in jellyfish can be used in producing biotechnological products, such as medicines and cosmetics.

Medicine

The venom in some jellyfish tentacles is being studied for use in producing painkillers or even cancer treatments.

Food

In countries like Japan and China, jellyfish are consumed as a delicacy, rich in protein and used in various dishes.
